My Story / Mi Historia

Hi, I'm Michelle

From café con pan to la vida balanceada

Growing up, nobody taught me what a balanced meal actually looked like. Breakfast was café con pan. Dinner was whatever was on the table — rice, beans, tortillas — all love, no nutrition education. I didn't know what a protein was, what a fat did, or why my body felt the way it felt.

In my early twenties, my body started talking. I was dealing with constant bloating, gut issues, and then something that truly scared me — I lost my period. My body was out of balance, and every diet I tried made it worse. Restricting didn't work. Cutting carbs didn't work. I felt completely disconnected from my own body.

That's when I decided to actually learn. I enrolled in a nutrition program and studied how food really works — hormones, gut health, metabolism, the whole picture. Then I applied everything to my own life, with the real foods I grew up eating. I lost 30 lbs, got my period back, healed my gut, and felt at home en mi cuerpo for the first time.

"I don't want you to start over. I want you to finally understand your body — con tu cultura, en tus términos."

La Vida Balanceada was built from that journey. Because I know what it's like to grow up without the information, to try everything and feel like nothing works — and to finally find real, lasting balance without giving up the foods or the life you love. That's exactly what I help you do.

And now I teach this method to other Latinas — so they don’t have to figure it out alone.
